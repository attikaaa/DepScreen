3. What is depression?
Depression is a constant feeling of sadness and loss of interest, which stops you from doing your normal activities.
4. What are the stages of depression?
There are three main stages of depression: mild depression, moderate depression, severe depression.

8. How can I seek help for depression?
If you are experiencing symptoms of depression, it's important to reach out to a mental health professional. You can contact a therapist, counselor, or psychiatrist for support and guidance.
9. Can lifestyle changes help manage depression?
Yes, adopting a healthy lifestyle can play a crucial role in managing depression. Regular exercise, balanced nutrition, proper sleep, and stress reduction techniques can contribute to overall well-being.
10. Are there support groups for people with depression?
Yes, there are various support groups and online communities where individuals facing depression can connect, share experiences, and provide mutual support.
11. How do I maintain good mental health?
Maintaining good mental health involves a combination of self-care practices, seeking professional support when needed, staying connected with loved ones, and engaging in activities that bring joy and fulfillment.
12. What is Mindfulness Meditation?
Mindfulness meditation is a practice that involves focusing the mind on the present moment. It can help reduce stress, improve concentration, and promote overall well-being.
