Dr.Shaini Suraj

Dr Shaini Suraj is originally trained as an Ayurvedic doctor, but profound fascination with mental health led her to pursue a career in Clinical psychology, demonstrating a unique and holistic approach to understanding and addressing mental health and well-being. She has done MA in Clinical psychology, pursuing PhD. She is an adolescent coach, regression therapist and relationship expert. She is PoSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) Certified. She has presented research papers in 5 International Conferences, 1 national conference. To her credit she has 25 copyrights and more than 25 publications. She is a member of various national and international organizations like IAAP , American Psychological Association, ICP International Council of Psychologists etc. She is the psychological Counsellor of YCCE and has changed the life of innumerable students by counseling.
